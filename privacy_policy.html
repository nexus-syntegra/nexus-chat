Nexus Chat Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Nexus Chat offers on-device AI processing using local language models. You can also optionally connect to external AI services. This policy explains what data stays on your device and what may be transmitted externally.

On-Device Processing

When using local AI models, your conversations are processed entirely on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third party.

External AI Services

Nexus Chat supports optional connections to external AI providers, including OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic, and API-compatible services. When you configure and use these services:

  • Your conversations are sent to the selected provider
  • Data handling is governed by that provider's privacy policy
  • You are responsible for reviewing the provider's terms before use

Web Search and Research Features

Nexus Chat includes optional web search and research agent features. When enabled:

  • Search queries and requests are sent to external services (e.g., DuckDuckGo, Brave Search, or configured endpoints)
  • Content from websites may be retrieved and processed

These features are disabled by default and require user activation.

Diagnostics and Analytics

We collect diagnostic data to maintain and improve the app, including:

  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics
  • General usage patterns

This data is collected via Firebase and is used for troubleshooting, bug fixes, and app improvements. Diagnostic data may include device information, app state at the time of an error, and interaction patterns. We do not use this data for advertising.

Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used for vision-language features. Images are processed according to your selected AI mode (local or external).
  • Microphone: Used for voice input. Audio is processed on-device for speech-to-text conversion.
  • Internet: Required for external AI services, web features, model downloads, cloud backup, subscriptions, and diagnostics.

Third-Party Services

Nexus Chat integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Crash reporting and analytics. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: Subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Google Drive: Optional backup/restore. Privacy Policy
  • Hugging Face: Optional model downloads. Privacy Policy
  • External AI Providers: When configured (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, others). See each provider's privacy policy.

Local Storage

Conversation history, settings, and downloaded models are stored locally on your device. You can delete this data through the app or by uninstalling.

Children's Privacy

Nexus Chat is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
